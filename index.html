Crafted with Love Since 2016

Mithas was born from a simple belief: ice cream should taste like ice cream, not like chemicals. We started in a small kitchen with pure milk, real fruits, and a passion for traditional Indian flavours.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of families across India with premium ice cream that celebrates our heritage while embracing modern taste preferences. Every scoop is a story of dedication, quality, and love.
