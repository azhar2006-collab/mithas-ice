About Mithas

Premium dairy ice cream, crafted the honest way

Mithas Ice Cream is made with real milk, butter, and fresh cream — never frozen desserts. Every product uses carefully selected ingredients to deliver rich taste, superior quality, and authentic dairy goodness from our FSSAI-registered, state-of-the-art manufacturing facility.

With modern technology ensuring hygienic production and consistent quality, Mithas reaches customers across a strong network of retail outlets spanning Chennai, Chengalpattu, Tiruvallur, and Kanchipuram.

How We Make It

Crafted Fresh, Every Morning

01
Farm-Fresh Milk
We collect full-fat milk daily from partner dairies within 200km of our facility.
02
Natural Infusion
Real saffron strands, whole fruits, and roasted nuts are steeped overnight for depth.
03
Slow Churning
Our churners run at low speed to create the dense, creamy texture Mithas is known for.
04
Flash Frozen
Sealed and frozen within minutes to lock in freshness before reaching your home.
